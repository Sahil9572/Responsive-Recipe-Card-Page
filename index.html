Italian Pasta

A classic Italian pasta dish with rich tomato sauce and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves

Preparation

Boil pasta until al dente, mix with sauce, and garnish with basil.

Fresh Garden Salad

A refreshing mix of lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices

Preparation

Mix all veggies in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and season to taste.

Cheese Pizza

Crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Preparation

Spread sauce on dough, add cheese, and bake until golden brown.
